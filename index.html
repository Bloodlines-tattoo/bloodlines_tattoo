Who we are

Meet Yehor.

Behind Bloodlines is Yehor — a self-driven artist with 2–3 years of dedicated practice, starting on artificial skin before moving to real clients. Every hour of that practice was intentional, building the precision and control that permanent work demands.

The portfolio on real skin is already growing fast. Yehor has completed a serious number of pieces across multiple styles — blackwork, realism, neo-trad, abstract — and is hungry to push further with every new client.

Bloodlines just opened its doors in Shannon, Co. Clare — and the pace has been relentless from day one. If you're looking for an artist who treats every piece like it's his most important work yet, you're in the right place.

What we do

Every style. One standard.

01
Blackwork

Bold, graphic, and timeless. Heavy black ink, solid fills, and strong contrast — pieces built to age well and stay powerful.

02
Realism

Photographic skin work. Animals, objects, textures — brought to life through depth, shadow, and precision shading. No portraits.

03
Black & Grey

Smooth gradients, soft shading, and subtle contrast. The timeless monochrome approach that works on any design.

04
Traditional & Neo-Trad

Thick outlines, saturated color, classic subject matter — updated with a contemporary eye and modern technique.

05
Abstract

Unpredictable compositions, expressive marks, and unconventional layouts. For those who want something that can't be replicated.

06
Cover-ups & Reworks

Breathing new life into old ink. Smart cover-up strategy that turns regret into something you'll love again.
